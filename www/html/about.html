About The Team

Welcome to the collaborative effort of a group of dedicated historians and innovative computer scientists based in the beautiful town of Gunnison, Colorado. Our mission is to forge a connection between the past and present by creating a dynamic, user-friendly website accessible to all members of the community. Together, we strive to bridge the gap between academia and the public, empowering students, community members, and tourists alike to discover and engage with the rich history that permeates every corner of our beloved Gunnison. By harnessing the latest technological tools and insights from the field of history, we aim to showcase the vibrant tapestry of cultural heritage that defines our town and its surrounding regions. So come along with us on a journey through time and space, as we uncover the stories, people, and events that have shaped Gunnison into the vibrant and welcoming community it is today.
